# DEMO VIDEO SCRIPT — ComplianceRadar
# Total time: 2:30-3:00 minutes
# Record with OBS or Loom. Screen recording + voice narration.
# DO NOT use slides. Show the working app only.

## 0:00 - 0:15 — HOOK (memorize this)

"63 million Indian MSMEs deal with 5 different government portals — MCA, GST, Income Tax, EPFO, Shops and Establishments. Each one has a different interface, different deadlines, and zero APIs. CAs charge lakhs to navigate these portals manually. I built ComplianceRadar to fix this."

## 0:15 - 0:35 — SHOW INPUT

[Show the app in browser. Type in a company name.]

"I enter a company name — let's use [COMPANY NAME]. I select the state of operation. And I select all 5 government portals to scan."

[Click "Run Compliance Scan"]

"One click. Five TinyFish agents launch in parallel."

## 0:35 - 1:20 — AGENTS RUNNING (the money shot)

[Show the agent activity feed with status indicators updating]

"You can see each agent navigating a different government portal right now. This one is on the MCA portal searching for company registration. This one is on the GST portal checking return filing status. Each portal has a completely different UI — the agents handle all of it."

[If using smart cut — narrate for 15-20 seconds of live agents, then say:]

"I'll let these finish — let me show you the completed results."

[Switch to completed results — either wait for real completion or use loadDemo()]

## 1:20 - 2:10 — RESULTS (walk through each section)

[Point to overall score]

"ComplianceRadar gives you an overall compliance health score — this business scores 72 out of 100."

[Point to stats row]

"4 portals show compliant status, but look at this..."

[Point to the red gap alert]

"THIS is the critical finding. No Shops and Establishments registration found in Maharashtra. If this company operates offices there, they're doing it without proper registration. That's a compliance gap they didn't know about."

[Scroll through portal cards]

"Each portal gets a risk score — green for compliant, yellow for approaching deadlines, red for critical issues. And each card shows the extracted data and a specific recommended action."

[Point to MCA card data]

"Look at the detail — company status, CIN number, last AGM date, last annual return filing, directors. All extracted directly from the MCA portal by the agent."

## 2:10 - 2:35 — SHOW EXTRAS

[Click Download Compliance Brief]

"You can download a complete compliance brief — hand this to your CA or use it for investor due diligence."

[Show the brief content briefly]

"Every portal, every status, every action item — in one document."

## 2:35 - 2:55 — CLOSE (memorize this)

"Indian government portals will never have APIs. The MCA portal will keep crashing. The GST portal will keep timing out. But 63 million MSMEs still need to know if they're compliant.

ComplianceRadar sends AI agents into these portals so business owners don't have to. Because the tiny fish of Indian business deserve the same compliance visibility that large corporations get from their expensive CA firms.

ComplianceRadar. Built with TinyFish."

## END

## TIPS FOR RECORDING:
1. Pre-run the scan 10 minutes before recording to make sure it works
2. Have loadDemo() ready as backup in browser console
3. Record 3-4 takes, pick the best one
4. Keep it raw and authentic — judges said "truth over polish"
5. Make sure your microphone is clear — audio quality matters
6. Don't read the script word-for-word — use it as a guide and speak naturally
7. Total time should be 2:30 to 3:00 minutes max
